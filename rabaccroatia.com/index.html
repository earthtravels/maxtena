Kiki Malo Apartments and Rooms in Rabac

Kiki Malo offers relaxing accommodations in beautiful Rabac. Whether you are an individual or couple looking for just a room or a family or large group requiring a suite and kitchenette, we can meet your needs. Our rooms offer lovely views of the Adriatic, as well as spacious balconies where you can rest and capture the cool sea breeze. The Apartments sit just a ten minute walk up from the gorgeous beaches below. Parking is available at the apartments as well as at the beaches.

What to See and Do in Rabac

Most visitors that come to Rabac are content to spend their holidays simply lying by the beach and enjoying the crystal clear, refreshing Adriatic. And we don’t blame them, since these are our favorite pastimes as well. Still, if you prefer a little more action, there are many options available. Snorkeling is another of our favorite activities, and if you come unprepared, many shops in Rabac offer affordable equipment, including masks, snorkels, and fins.
