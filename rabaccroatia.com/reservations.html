THE RABAC RESERVATION

The Rabac offers spacious self-catering apartments and rooms with very large terraces and stunning sea views. The apartments are located in the former fishing village of Rabac, known today as “the Pearl of Kvarner Bay.” The town of Rabac offers wonderful beaches, restaurants with local Istrian cuisine and various sporting activities.

Reservations and Policies

You may cancel reservations up until seven days prior to your arrival date for a refund of your payment less a €50.00 cancellation fee per reserved apartment. If you cancel your reservation or shorten your stay less than seven days prior to your arrival, you will be charged the full amount of the reservation, unless the apartment can be re-rented. You can cancel your reservation by email, telephone or through therabac.com.

  • Check-out time is 11 a.m.
