THE RABAC

Built and opened in 2001, our goal is to provide travelers visiting Rabac and Labin quiet, simple and relaxing accommodations at a very affordable price. We believe money is best spent on things like ice cream, boat rides, wine tastings, scuba diving trips, souvenirs, restaurants, and, most definitely, not on overpriced and undermanaged hotels. We will do everything we can to ensure you have all that you need in your apartment or room to keep your mind focused on the more important things, like lying on the beach, spending time with family, or hiking along the beautifully scenic trails. If you need a kitchen to cook a delicious dinner, no problem—all of our apartments have one. If you think coffee is best enjoyed in the morning while you savor the view of Rabac or gaze at dista¬nt boats skimming the Adriatic, then just step out onto your private balcony. Providing quiet, simple convenience to our guests at an affordable price is why we opened The Rabac, and why our guests keep coming back year after year. Make your vacation the best it can be and stay with us!

OFFICE LOCATION

Our main office is conveniently located on the ground floor of The Rabac. You can find us there Monday through Friday from 9AM to 9PM, and on both Saturday and Sunday from 9AM until 6PM. Outside of office hours, we can always be reached by telephone, through our contact page, or by email. We are here to make your vacation the best it can possibly be, so please do not hesitate to drop by our office or get in contact with us for any reason.
