MUSEUMS

The National Museum of Labin

The National Museum of Labin was founded in 1960 and it is located in the Palace Battiala Lazzarini. The most interesting feature of this museum is the Lapidarium that has valuable Roman monuments from 1st, 2nd and 3rd century BC. One can also find a collection of sacral artworks as well as a collection of pictures by Croatian national artists, as well as local artists from Labin. Tools and photos of the mines that lie beneath Labin are also featured within the museum.
