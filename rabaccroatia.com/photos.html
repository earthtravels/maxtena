The Rabac

Quiet, simple and relaxing rooms and apartments nestled in beautiful Rabac, Croatia. The Rabac overlooks the Adriatic Sea and is only a few minutes from historic Labin.


Rabac

Rabac, a Croatian village in Istra, is a large resort town on Kvarner Bay, just southeast of Labin. Long a small fishing port of just three dozen homes, Rabac has grown in recent years into a resort town with numerous villas and apartment buildings. Rabac can accommodate as many as 11,000 visitors who wish to relax in its quiet charm and beauty.


Labin

In the eastern part of Istra, only three kilometers from the sea lies the medieval town of Labin, today a town known for its unique vibrant feel that features numerous galleries and artists. The most fascinating sight is the central town square with the perfectly preserved loggia and main town gate of St. Flora from the 16th century. Above the gate is the town’s coat of arms and Venetian lion, the symbol of Venetian rule. Discover relics from times passed, like the large Austrian cannon or stone etchings from ancient Roman times as you stroll through the town.
