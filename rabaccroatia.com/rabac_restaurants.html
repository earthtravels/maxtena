RESTAURANTS

Restaurant Due Fratelli

Montozi 5
52220, Labin
Tel: ++385 52 853 577

One of the best known restaurants in historic Labin, Due Fratelli serves traditional Istrian cuisine. Some of the most popular choices at the restaurant include freshly caught seafood and meals prepared on their outdoor grill. Due Fratelli also serves high quality Istrian wines, as well as a large selection of desserts.

Reservations are recommended on the weekends.

Open for Lunch and Dinner.

Restaurant Capitano

Rabac

We recommend the fresh calamari as an entrée; it comes either grilled or fried in a golden batter. The Cevapcici is also delicious. Every meal comes with a complimentary glass of traditional brandy.

Reservations are not required, but are recommended on weekend evenings.

Open for Lunch and Dinner.

Restaurant Miramare

Rabac

This cozy restaurant has wonderful views of the sea and sits close to the busy riva. You can enjoy a wide array of traditional Croatian foods here. Be sure to try the fresh mussels cooked in wine sauce when you stop by.

Reservations are not required, but are recommended on weekend evenings.

Open for Lunch and Dinner.

Bella Vista

Rabac

Bella Vista is situated on the riva and is less than 15 meters from the sea. This restaurant offers great hand tossed pizzas, seafood dishes, and traditional Istrian dishes. Here you can enjoy your meal with friends and family as you look out onto the sea.

Reservations are not required, but are recommended on weekend evenings.

Open for Lunch and Dinner.
